DataShake

Your No-Code platform to automate ETL workflows visually, intuitively, and without writing code

What is DataShake?

DataShake is a No-Code ETL solution that enables you to transform, clean, and migrate data between multiple sources and targets with just a few clicks. Its friendly interface empowers even non-technical users to build powerful and flexible data pipelines.

Key Features

  • Direct extraction from relational databases.
  • Field transformations for both text and numeric types (concatenation, replacement, arithmetic operations, casing).
  • Automatic aggregations such as group counts and averages.
  • Schema synchronization between source and target datasets.
  • Designed to support various scenarios: HR, finance, logistics, and more.

Use Case Example: Employee Management

This real-world example demonstrates how DataShake can be used in a Human Resources scenario:

  • Insert new employee records into a source database.
  • Transform fields such as names and departments (e.g., uppercase conversion or text concatenation).
  • Perform numeric operations on salaries and work hours (addition, multiplication, etc.).
  • Automatically generate summaries such as employee counts per department and average salaries.

This case illustrates how DataShake can automate internal processes without needing developers.

Who Is This Tool For?

  • Data analysts seeking to automate workflows without coding.
  • Business teams needing to integrate diverse data sources.
  • Startups and companies aiming to reduce ETL implementation time.

Getting Started

DataShake is built on Flask and SQLAlchemy, but requires no coding interaction. Just deploy the app, load your data, and begin transforming it visually.
